Anime, the lost Artform

Hello, and welcome to Kai's weeb corner!

Here, I will introduce you to my top 5 anime picks, and give you a little insight in what anime is all about. Maybe I will even teach you some terminology within the anime community, or inspire you to find something that might peak your interest.

But first of all, what is anime?

Typically, most people use the term to refer to hand‐drawn and computer‐generated animation originating from Japan, such as Pok√©mon, Naruto, Yu‐Gi‐Oh! and Dragon Ball Z to name a few. However, in Japan, the term is used as an abbreviation of the english term 'Animation' and is a general term for anything that has been animated, regardless of origin.
